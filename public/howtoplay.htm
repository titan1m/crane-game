  1. Select a level (Easy / Medium / Hard) from Home.
  2. Use Arrow Keys to move the crane: Left, Right, Up, Down.
  3. Catch the red objects to score points.
  4. You have limited lives based on level: Easy-3, Medium-2, Hard-1.
  5. Game ends when timer runs out or lives are 0.
  6. Your score is saved in MongoDB and shown in Leaderboard.
  7. Check your high score in Profile page.
