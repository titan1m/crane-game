How to Play

  1. Create an Account: Register with your email and username

  4. Answer Questions: Select the correct answer from multiple choices
  5. Earn Points: Get points for each correct answer
  6. Climb Leaderboard: Compete with other players

Scoring System

  • No penalty for wrong answers
