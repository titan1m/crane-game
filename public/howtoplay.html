How to Play

  • Select a difficulty level: Easy, Medium, or Hard.
  • Answer quiz questions correctly to earn points.
  • Each correct answer increases your score. Consecutive correct answers give streak bonuses.
  • Use lifelines wisely: 50-50 (removes two wrong options), Skip, Extra Time (+5s).
  • You have limited lives. Wrong answers or running out of time reduces lives.
  • Check your achievements and leaderboard to track progress.
